Mince Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is the ultimate 'cosy food' - perfect for filling up hungry tummies after a busy day. This easy beef lasagne is made using a handful of basic ingredients (many of which you will already have at home)This easy beef lasagne is made using a handful of basic ingredients (many of which you will already have at home)

Ingredients

  • Diced onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Beef mince (aka ground beef for the Americans)
  • Cheese (the more the better)
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef stock
  • Dried Italian herbs
  • Cheeses - mozarella and parmesan
  • Lasagna sheets (preferably fresh)

Recipe Steps

  1. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to a large frying pan. Saut√© the onion and garlic until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the beef mince and break up clumps with a spoon. Cook until the mince has browned.
  3. Add the tomato paste, tomatoes, beef stock, Italian herbs and salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil and then simmer. Stir occasionally for 15-20 min.
  5. Once the sauce is ready, add a layer of sauce to a prepared baking dish. Alternate between layers of sauce and pasta sheets until the sauce and sheets are used up.
  6. Finish with a layer of mozarella cheese and a little parmesan.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until cooked through with golden cheese.
